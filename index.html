I am a postdoctoral researcher in the team Combinatoire et Interactions of the department Combinatoire et Algorithmique in the Laboratoire Bordelais de Recherche Informatique (LaBRI)), at Université de Bordeaux .

Research interests

I focus on enumerative, geometric and algebraic combinatorics.
Objects I usually interact with are posets, (semidistributive) lattices, arc diagrams and weak orders on permutations; polytopes (permutohedra, associahedra, quotientopes...) and polytopal fans; combinatorial Hopf algebras.
