I am a third year PhD. student under the supervision of Jean-Christophe Novelli, in the Combinatoire Algébrique et Calcul Symbolique team (COMBI), in the Laboratoire d'Informatique Gaspard Monge (LIGM), at Université Gustave Eiffel (UGE).

Research interests

I focus on geometric and algebraic combinatorics, more precisely on polytopes, polytopal fans, weak order on permutations, combinatorial Hopf algebras, integer posets and lattices.
